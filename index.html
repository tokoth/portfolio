About Me


I hold a Bachelor of Applied Science in GeoInformatics and have worked with both public and private organizations in Eastern Africa. I have experience working as part of a team with a thorough understanding and application of GIS technology for a variety of projects, as well as experience learning and applying new technologies as needed.

Projects


A selection of recent projects I've worked on.

Bike Mapping

A project looking into processing, cleaning, and visualizing huge open datasets, by exploring NYC Bike Share Data. Huge credits to Citi Bike System Data New York City, for publishing there bike rides data, this was used as a datasource for the project.
An interactive map depicting the most popular bike stations in 2018, 2019, and 2020. You can play about with the map and see what year you're interested in.

Topi Tjukanov's #30DayMapChallenge

Taking part in this challenge provided me with an excellent opportunity to learn, experiment with different mapping approaches, tools, as well as convince/prove my cartographic skillset to myself (and possibly others) in the form of a portfolio.
Mapping tools and software’s used; QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, Python, Blender, Aerialod, and GIMP.
